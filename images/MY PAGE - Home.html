About Me

A college student in a journey of self exploration and world beauty capturing adventure by using photography. I believe that a single photo can build up strong emotions and reactions from someone. Not only images of a person but also through nature and other living things. That is why for me, photography is more than a work, it is a form of art and self- expression.
